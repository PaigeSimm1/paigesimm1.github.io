Paige Simmons

GAME DEVELOPER

Hello! I’m Paige Simmons, a sophomore at Boise State University majoring in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile. I focus on gameplay systems, mechanic design, and developing immersive experiences across platformers, rhythm games, and AR projects. My skill set includes Unity, Unreal Engine 5, C#, JavaScript, Lua, 2D/3D asset integration, 3D modeling, and music production.


Prior to attending Boise State, I gained industry experience as a successful Roblox developer and sound engineer, blending creativity with strong technical foundations.


Why Game Development?

Growing up with an older brother, video games were always a huge part of my life. Some of my earliest memories are of playing games with my family and sharing those moments together. I was also surrounded by development at home. My dad is a software engineer, and I was always curious about the projects he worked on and the code behind them.


I’m someone who asks questions when I need to and takes initiative to learn independently. I love picking up new skills and constantly work to grow as a developer. Throughout my projects, I’ve contributed both as a team member and as a Team Lead, and I enjoy collaborating with others to bring ideas to life.


Being able to turn my visions into something real and playable is incredibly meaningful and fun! Game development brings together everything I’m passionate about: storytelling, design, technology, and creativity. I’ve always known this is what I want to do.
